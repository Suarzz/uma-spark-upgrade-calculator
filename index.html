Spark Upgrade Calculator

This calculator is used to know the chance of a particular pink factor activating on both legacy events on your run. The factor used is "Turf" but applies for every pink factor (track/distance/style).

How to use? If you want to know the chance of your trainee activating the "medium" spark on a run (and hence upgrading their aptitude to S) select the number of stars of your legacies, on their respective slots.
If for example your legacy 1 parent has a different pink factor than the one you want to calculate (medium in this case) select the option "no spark".
